What is AIO and how is it different from regular SEO?+
AIO stands for AI Optimization. Traditional SEO targets Google's 10 blue links. AIO goes further — it optimizes your content to be cited, quoted, and recommended by AI platforms like ChatGPT, Google AI Overviews, Perplexity, and Copilot. As AI-driven search grows, AIO ensures your business gets found in both traditional and AI-generated results simultaneously.
What is Generative Engine Optimization (GEO)?+
GEO is the practice of structuring your content so AI language models reference your business as an authority. When someone asks ChatGPT "who is the best advertising agency in Castle Rock?" — GEO is what puts your business in that answer. It requires deep topical authority, structured data, and content AI systems trust and choose to cite.

What makes a website actually generate leads?+
Five things that are all non-negotiable: speed under 2.5 seconds, a crystal-clear value proposition above the fold, multiple conversion touchpoints (phone, form, chat), trust signals (reviews, credentials, guarantees), and SEO-structured content that attracts the right visitors in the first place. Most websites fail on at least three of these.
What is programmatic display advertising?+
Programmatic display uses AI and real-time bidding to show your ads to precisely targeted people across millions of websites and apps. Unlike traditional display that targets websites, programmatic targets specific people — by behavior, intent, location, and device. You reach the right person at exactly the right moment in their decision process.
How does PPC work for Castle Rock businesses?+
PPC lets you appear at the top of search results immediately. You only pay when someone clicks. We build geo-targeted campaigns that capture high-intent Castle Rock searches.
